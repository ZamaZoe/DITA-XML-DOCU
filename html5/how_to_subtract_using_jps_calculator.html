How To Subtract Using JPS Calculator

Here you will find instructions on making subtraction on JPS calculator.

To successfully subtract numbers, you must know what is subtraction.
  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the minus (-) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal button.
The results from the calculation is displayed.
Example:

You want to subtract 2 from 3.The equation will look like : 3-2=1. Enter 3. Press minus(-). Enter 2. Press equals(=).

The answer will appear on the screen.
